Changes of international students in the U.S. around the epidemic

Chapter 2 Data sources

The aim of this project is to analyze the changes in the population of international students studying in the United States from various countries in recent years. The data was collected from All Places of Origin, Open Door, which is sponsored by the U.S. Government and Institute of International Education. We selected international students for from 1949 to 2020 from various regions, including Africa, Asia, Europe, Latin America & Caribbean, Middle East, Oceania.

2.3 Issue of Data set

2.3.1 For missing Value

There are a lot of NAs in the dataset, but there are some that do not make sense. Because the data source is in Excel format and separating it with blank lines is a common Excel method.
